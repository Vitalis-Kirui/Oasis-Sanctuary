ABOUT US

If you ask directions from someone coming from our vicinity, you will hear just "Sanctuary". We accept this short and precise name but it also good to know our full name. Our official name are Kipsigis Oasis Sanctuary and Culture. From this name, a lot about us becomes self-explanatory.

Kipsigis Oasis Sanctuary and Culture is a private entity or rather a private conservational project. We are also legally registered under Bomet County Government. We have a lot going on in the site but we major in some specific areas.

Areas of major;

  • Wildlife Conservation
  • Culture Conservation
  • Conferencing and Accomondations

... these are just a few of our goals and services we offer.
