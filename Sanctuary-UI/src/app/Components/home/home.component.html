About Kipsigis Sanctuary

Kipsigis Oasis Sanctuary and Culture is our official and registered name. We are a private wildlife and cultural conservation entity located in Bomet county, Chebunyo ward.

Wildlife

From our name, we basically conserve wildlife and culture, Wildlife in the sense that, we conserve wild animals and natural environment/natural species. As it stands, we barely have any wild animals in there but it's an achievable dream within our reach in the near future, we have taken major steps to realize this dream. So far our efforts have earned us a Zebra which we are forever grateful to Kenya Wildlife Service for making this happen.

Culture

Culture is another important component in our site and that takes a lot of weight when it comes to our primary goals and targets. We have majored in this as well and used a significant amount of our resources to realize this.Inside the site, we have a separate part of us that majors in culture conservation. We try to accomondate all the cultures as much as possible.
