CULTURE CENTRE

Culture Center is a department in our sanctaury. Basically it's a specialized department for cultural conservation, cultural activities and anything about culture that we currently offer.

It's one of the hotspot place inside our sanctuary, It has attracted a lot of attention across the country. If you haven't visited us, you may have seen videos, photos from this very place. A lot of videos from a number of singers have filmed many of their songs here.

This is where clients get a chance to dress in different cultural attires at small fee, which will definitely be worth it if you ask me. It's an experience like no other. You will see more of this site when you visit our gallery as most of the pics are from this area.
